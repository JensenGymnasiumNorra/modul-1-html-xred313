The second article

Pizza Declared Official Vegetable in Sweden

In a shocking move, Sweden’s National Food Agency has announced that pizza will now count as one daily serving of vegetables.
The decision was based on the "value of tomato sauce and occasional toppings like peppers.”
Nutritionists are divided, while school cafeterias are already preparing triple-cheese menus.

Ikea Announces Flat-Pack Spaceship

Ikea revealed today its first space exploration kit, the FÄRGRIK-3000, a flat-pack spaceship designed for “affordable interplanetary travel.”
The manual reportedly contains 8,542 steps and a single Allen key. Early testers claim the ship reached orbit successfully,
though one astronaut admitted:“We may have one screw left over.”
